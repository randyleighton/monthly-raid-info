Pokémon GO: Kyurem and Fusion Guide

Kyurem is a legendary Ice/Dragon-type Pokémon known for fusing with Zekrom or Reshiram using DNA Splicers and Fusion Energy.

Fusion Basics

  • Kyurem is the base; inherits IVs, level, shiny/lucky status.
  • Requires: DNA Splicers, 1,000 Fusion Energy (Volt for Black, Blaze for White), 30 Kyurem Candy + 30 partner candies.
  • Reversible for free; refusion costs energy again.

Black Kyurem (Dragon/Electric)

  • Fuse with Zekrom + Volt Fusion Energy.
  • Glaciate → Freeze Shock (Ice/Electric).
  • Adventure Effect: Freezes encounters (easier catches) for 10 min (5k Stardust + 5 Candy).

White Kyurem (Dragon/Flying)

  • Fuse with Reshiram + Blaze Fusion Energy.
  • Glaciate → Ice Burn (Ice/Fire).
  • Adventure Effect: Slows catch ring for Excellent Throws, 10 min.

Getting Glaciate on Kyurem

Yes, even if not on catch: Use Elite Charged TM (ECTM). Event raids/Special Research also give Glaciate Kyurem.[web:11][web:13][web:18]

Fusion Tip

Teach Glaciate before fusing—fused forms can't TM it. Prioritize high-IV Glaciate Kyurem; energy from Black/White raids.[web:14][web:11]
